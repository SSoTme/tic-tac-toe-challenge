Elevator Pitch

This document contains about 15-20 page, highly detailed specification - like, for an Enterprise version of 3x3 Tic-Tac-Toe. 2 developers compete to create the best implementation of the spec. Then everything changes and the developers have to adapt to those changes.

Logistics

  1. 2 Developers, using the tools, languages, libraries of their choice are asked to implement the game of Tic-Tac-Toe designed by this specification
  2. 80% through the agreed upon challenge time, the following rules will be changed by the judges.
    • The Golden Rule - There is 1 rule which will not change - this is a 3x3 game of Tic Tac Toe. This is not connect 4 or connect n. It is 3x3 and there are explicitly 9, named cells.
      ... Other than that - everything's up for grabs.
    • Language such as Game On and Game Over may change
    • Levels of play may be added or removed
    • Strategies may be added, removed or changed
    • Winning conditions might be changed
    • Novel new features/requests might be added...
    • Colors/Labels and other text, they're all subject to change
  3. Each competitor then has the final 20% of the challenge time to adjust & incorporate as many of the changes as possible.
