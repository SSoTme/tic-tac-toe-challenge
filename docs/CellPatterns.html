Winning Patterns

There are 11 winning patterns defined.

Diagonal Win

Win along either diagonal

Row1

The firist (top) row in the puzzle

Row2

The 2nd (middle) row in the puzzle

Row3

The 3rd (bottom) row in the puzzle.

Column1

The 1st (leftmost)column in the puzzle.

Column2

The 2nd (middle) column in the puzzle.

Column3

The 3rd (rightmost) column in the puzzle.

Left Right Diagonal

The diagonal from top left to bottom right.

Right Left Diagonal

The Diagonal from top right to bottom left.

Edge Win

Starting with the top row, 3 additional rotates will find all edge wins.

Middle Win

Starting with the middle row selected, a single rotations finds both middle wins

Possible Win Matching Strategies

There are 6 general patterns which are useful for a variety of strategies outlined.

Two In A Row Top

Two marks top left, top middle with top right empty - and then a full rotation/flip scheme.

Two In A Row Middle

Two in a row on the middle row, right side empty - finds all positions with 3 rotations.

Two In A Row Diagonal

Two in a row on the diagonal, with the third cell empty - finds all positions with 3 rotations.

Two Outside Top

Top left and top right filled, middle empty - finds all positions with 3 rotations.

Two Outside Middle

Middle left and right, center empty - 1 rotation finds the one variation of the starting pattern.

Two Outside Diagonal

Top left and bottom right with middle empty - 1 rotation finds the one variation of the starting pattern.
